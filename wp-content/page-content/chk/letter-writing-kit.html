Step 3: Submit omw leta

Email

Email ese ewe alen mi tou me mi common. Laplap legislator me council member ra list eu public email address won ar official page. Use eu clear subject line: "Constituent letter regarding [topic]" ika "Written testimony — [bill number or agenda item]."

Mail

Physical leta a tongeni effective, auchea ren state legislator. Address ewe envelope ngeni ewe representative an Capitol ika district office. Use ewe address mi list won ar official page. Allow extra tien ren physical mail — ka sap mail ewe ran me mwen eu hearing.

Written testimony ren Hawaii Legislature hearing

  • Nom won capitol.hawaii.gov
  • Kunok ewe bill ika hearing ka mochen comment usun
  • Katon ewe "Submit Testimony" link won ewe hearing ika measure page
  • Written testimony a typically due 24 hour me mwen ewe hearing
  • Meinisin submitted testimony a wiis eu part en ewe public record

County council written comment

  • Eu me eu county a wor an submission process — katon ewe specific council website ren instruction
  • Ren Honolulu, written testimony a tongeni tien submit ren email ngeni ewe council clerk me mwen ewe meeting
  • Deadline ra vary — katon ewe specific agenda ren submission cutoff tien

Public comment period

  • State agency ra post proposed rule me environmental document ren public comment
  • Comment period ese typically 30 ngeni 45 ran me ra publish non Hawaii Administrative Rules me official notice
  • Submit ren ewe method mi list non ewe specific notice — usually email ika mail ngeni ewe agency

Tip ren effective leta

  • Wiis specific. "Ngang u nom eeniman block seni ewe proposed development site me ikkei met u katon" a mi powerful leniap seni vague general statement.
  • Wiis concise. Eu ngeni ruuanu page a ngengen. Representative ra ale nap leta — eu focused leta a mi likely kopwe ridini meinisin.
  • Wiis respectful. Nge ka disagree strongly, eu respectful tone a mi effective leniap. Representative ese aramas.
  • Sign omw iten me include omw address. Anonymous leta ra carry tikitik weight. Ika ka titi ngeni eu state legislator, ra kana kopwe silei pwe ka eu constituent non ar district.
  • Titi omw leta. Form leta ra count nge individual leta mi share personal experience a mi impactful leniap.
  • Fangeni won tien. Ren hearing, submit written testimony at least 24 hour me mwen. Ren public comment period, ka sap monien tori ewe iteiten ran.
