Ua kūkulu ʻia ʻo civi.me e nā kānaka o Hawaiʻi, no nā kānaka o Hawaiʻi. Inā he ʻelima minuke a i ʻole he ʻelima hola i kēlā me kēia pule kou manawa, aia nō kekahi ala e kōkua ai. Eia nā ala i loaʻa nei — a ʻo nā mea hou aku, e hiki mai ana.

E hele a komo i nā hālāwai kūʻokoʻa

ʻO ke ala pololei loa e komo ai ʻo ia ka hele. Aia ma Hawaiʻi ma mua o 400 papa, komisina, a me kōmike e hoʻoholo ana i nā mea like ʻole — mai ka hale noho a me ka ʻāina a hiki i ka hoʻonaʻauao a me nā kulekele kaiapuni. Ua wehe ʻia kēlā me kēia hālāwai no ka lehulehu — a ʻo ka hapa nui, e aloha ana i ka hōʻike lehulehu.

ʻAʻole pono ʻoe e lilo i mea akamai. ʻAʻole pono e hoʻomākaukau i haʻiʻōlelo. E hele wale, e hoʻolohe, a e kamaʻilio i kou manaʻo ke makemake ʻoe. He koho kāu e like pū me ko hai aku o ka papa.

E hoʻomaka pehea

  • E nānā i nā hālāwai e hiki mai ana — e ʻimi i nā hālāwai ma o ke kumuhana, kōmike, a i ʻole ka lā
  • E kākoʻo i nā hoʻolaha — e loaʻa nā leka aʻo i ka wā e pāpā ʻia ai nā hālāwai hou no nā kōmike āu e mālama ana
  • E heluhelu i ka papa hana — aia ma kēlā me kēia ʻaoʻao hālāwai ka papa hana a me nā palapala e loaʻa ana, i ʻike ai ʻoe i ka mea e kūkākūkā ʻia ana
  • E hoʻouna i ka hōʻike — ʻae ana ka hapa nui o nā kōmike i ka hōʻike palapala ma o ka leka uila ma mua o ka hālāwai, no laila hiki iā ʻoe ke komo ʻoiai ʻaʻole hiki iā ʻoe ke hele piha

ʻO ka hapa nui o nā hālāwai, loaʻa ka nānā kikohoʻe ma o Zoom, no laila hiki iā ʻoe ke komo mai kahi aku. E nānā i ka ʻaoʻao kikoʻī o kēlā me kēia hālāwai no ka loulou.

E nānā i nā hālāwai e hiki mai ana   E loaʻa hoʻolaha

He hou ʻoe i nā hālāwai kūʻokoʻa? ʻO kā mākou mau alakaʻi e pili ana i ka hele i ka hālāwai a me ke ʻano o ka hōʻike e alakaʻi ana iā ʻoe i nā kaʻina hana a pau.

E lilo i ʻElele civi.me

ʻO nā ʻElele ka puʻuwai o ka noho kaiāulu o civi.me. Hoʻokipa lākou i nā hanana, kaʻana like i nā kumuwaiwai, a kōkua i ko lākou mau hoalauna i ka maopopo o ke ʻano e komo ai i nā hoʻoholo kūʻokoʻa.

He kuleana kokua kēia — ʻaʻole pono ka ʻike mua. Inā e mālama ana ʻoe i kou kaiāulu a makemake e kōkua i nā kānaka e komo ai, ua kūpono ʻoe.

Nā hana a nā ʻElele

  • E hoʻokipa i nā ʻaha kākau leka a me nā hanana komo kīwila ma kou wahi noho
  • E kaʻana like i nā mea lawelawe a kumuwaiwai o civi.me me nā hoaaloha, ʻohana, a me nā hui kaiāulu
  • E hāʻawi i ka manaʻo e alakaʻi ai i ka ulu ʻana o civi.me
  • E hoʻopili iā mākou i nā kaiāulu āu i kipa ʻole ai mākou

Ka poʻe āu e ʻimi ana

E makemake nui ana mākou e lohe mai nā haumāna o ke kula kiʻekiʻe a me ke kulanui, nā mea hoʻonohonoho kaiāulu, nā lālā papa kaiāulu, a me ka mea hana ma kekahi hui moʻomeheu a kaiāulu paha. ʻAʻole pono ka ʻike kīwila mua — ʻo ka makemake wale nō a me ke pilina i kou kaiāulu.

Ke kūkulu nei mākou i nā pilina me UH Mānoa, nā kulanui kaiāulu, a me nā papahana hana lawelawe o nā kula kiʻekiʻe. Inā ʻoe he haumāna e ʻimi ana i kahi papahana koʻikoʻi, he kūpono kēia.

Nā loaʻa nāu

  • He pūʻolo lawelawe ʻElele me nā mea āpau e pono ai e hoʻokipa ai i nā hanana
  • Nā haʻawina liʻiliʻi hanana e uhi ai i nā kumukūʻai ʻai a me ka hale ("e hāʻawi i ka pizza")
  • Ke kākoʻo mai ka hui civi.me
  • Ka ʻoluʻolu o ka hana ʻana i kou kaiāulu i ʻike hou a komo nui

E hoʻokipa i ka ʻaha kākau leka

ʻO ka ʻaha kākau leka, ʻo ia nō: e hui mai nā kānaka, e aʻo i kekahi mea e hana ana ma ko lākou kaiāulu, a e kākau i nā leka i ko lākou mau luna. Hiki ke liʻiliʻi — he poe hoa paha ma ka pākaukau — a i ʻole he hanana nui kaiāulu ma ka hale waihona puke a i ʻole ke kikowaena kaiāulu.

Hāʻawi ʻo civi.me i nā kāleka palapala, ka ʻike hoʻopili, a me nā alakaʻi hoʻouna. ʻO ʻoe ka mea hāʻawi i ka ikaika a me nā kānaka. Hiki iā mākou ke kōkua i ka uhi ʻana i ke kumukūʻai ʻai no nā hanana i hoʻokipa ʻia e nā ʻElele.

E loaʻa ka alakaʻi hoʻokipa hanana

E lilo i hoa hana

Ke ʻimi nei ʻo civi.me i nā hui kaiāulu, nā hui moʻomeheu, nā hale waihona puke, nā kulanui, a me nā hui pono ʻole i ka pāʻoihana e like ana ko lākou koi me kā mākou i ka komo kīwila. Ua wehe mākou i ka wahi no ka hoʻokipa like i nā hanana, ka kaʻana like i nā kumuwaiwai, a me ka kūkulu like i nā mea lawelawe.

E pili mākou i nā hui ʻaʻole kākoʻo pāleka wale nō. ʻAʻole mākou e ʻae i nā kālā a me nā pilina hoa hana e lawe mai ana i ka mana hoʻoponopono a i ʻole he pahuhopu kālaiʻāina.

Ko mākou makemake nui e hoʻopili aku ai

  • Nā hale waihona puke a me nā kikowaena kaiāulu e hiki ai ke hoʻokipa i nā hanana
  • Nā hui moʻomeheu e lawelawe ana i nā kaiāulu like ʻole o Hawaiʻi
  • Nā hui aupuni haumāna kulanui a me nā hui kīwila
  • Nā hui nūpepa a paʻipai e pili ana i ka ʻikepili kīwila
  • Nā papahana ʻenehana kīwila ʻē aʻe e kūkulu ana i nā mea lawelawe pili

E kamaʻilio e pili ana i ka pilina hoa hana

E hoʻolaha aku

ʻO ka mea maʻalahi loa hiki iā ʻoe ke hana ʻo ia ka haʻi aku i kekahi. Inā ʻoe e ʻike ana i kekahi hoalauna, haumāna, a i ʻole lālā kaiāulu e mālama ana i ka mea e hana ana ma ko lākou kaiāulu akā ʻaʻole i ʻike i ka wahi e hoʻomaka ai — e hoʻouna iā lākou mai nei.

ʻAʻohe hoʻolaha ma civi.me. Ua ulu mākou ma o ka ʻōlelo a ka waha. ʻOi aku ka koʻikoʻi o kou ʻōlelo hoʻāpono ma mua o kekahi haʻawina hoʻolaha.
