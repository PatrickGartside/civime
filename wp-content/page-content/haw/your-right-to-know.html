Hāʻawi ka māhele kānāwai o Hawaiʻi iā ʻoe i nā kuleana kikoʻī e nānā i nā moʻolelo aupuni a e hele i nā hālāwai lehulehu. ʻElua kānāwai ke kumu o ka ākea aupuni ma ka mokuʻāina — a ua mālama ʻia lāua e ka hale hoʻokahi. E wehewehe ana kēia ʻaoʻao i kā lāua hana, i ka hoʻohana ʻana iā lāua, a me ka mea e hana ai ke pilikia.

He wehewehe ʻōlelo maʻalahi kēia. No ka kikokikona kānāwai piha, e nānā i nā kumuhana i hoʻopili ʻia ma lalo. No ke kōkua i kekahi kūlana kikoʻī, e hoʻokamaʻāina i ka Office of Information Practices.

Ka Sunshine Law — kāu kuleana e hele i nā hālāwai lehulehu

ʻO ka Sunshine Law (Hawaii Revised Statutes Mokuna 92) ka māhele kānāwai hālāwai wehe o Hawaiʻi. Wahi āna, ke hālāwai ka papa, ke komisina, a i ʻole ka kaunihela aupuni e kūkākūkā ana i nā hana aupuni, he kuleana kāu e hele ai.

Ka mea i koi ʻia e ka Sunshine Law

  • ʻŌlelo aʻo ma mua. Pono e hoʻolaha ʻia nā papa hana hālāwai he ʻeono lā kalenaka ma mua o ka hālāwai.
  • Nā papa hana i paʻi ʻia. Pono e helu ʻia ma ka ʻōlelo aʻo nā kumuhana āpau e kūkākūkā ʻia. ʻAʻole hiki i ka papa ke kūkākūkā i kekahi mea i komo ole ma ka papa hana.
  • ʻŌlelo hōʻike o ka lehulehu. He kuleana kāu e hōʻike ana no nā kumuhana o ka papa hana ma nā hālāwai lehulehu.
  • Nā koho pāloka lehulehu. Pono e hana ʻia nā koho pāloka ma ke ʻano ākea. ʻAʻohe koho pāloka huna.
  • Nā moʻolelo i paʻi ʻia. Pono ka papa e mālama i kahi moʻolelo palapala o ka mea i hana ʻia, a he moʻolelo lehulehu kēlā moʻolelo.

Ka mea i uhi ʻole ʻia e ka Sunshine Law

  • Nā ʻahaʻōlelo maʻamau kahi i hana ʻole ʻia ai nā hoʻoholo a ʻoi aku ka uʻi o ka quorum (ka hapa nui o nā lālā)
  • Nā hālāwai limahana o loko — pili ka māhele kānāwai i nā papa a nā komisina, ʻaʻole i nā hana o kēlā me kēia lā o ka ʻoihana
  • Ka ʻAha Kau Māmala Kānāwai — ʻO ka Hawaii State Legislature ke kānāwai kona iho, ʻaʻole ka Sunshine Law (ʻoiai ua wehe ʻia nā hālāwai ʻaha i ka lehulehu)

Ka mea nāna e mālama

ʻO ka Office of Information Practices (OIP) ka mea nāna e mālama ana i ka Sunshine Law. Inā manaʻo ʻoe ua pani pono ʻole ʻia kekahi hālāwai i ka lehulehu, a i ʻole ua kūkākūkā ʻia kekahi kumuhana papa hana me ka ʻole o ka ʻōlelo aʻo kūpono, hiki iā ʻoe ke hoʻokamaʻāina i OIP (e nānā ma lalo).

Nā loulou: HRS Mokuna 92 — kikokikona piha · Nā alakaʻi OIP no ka Sunshine Law

Makemake ʻoe e ʻike i ka Sunshine Law e hana ana? E heluhelu i kā mākou alakaʻi no ke hele ʻana i kahi hālāwai lehulehu.

UIPA — kāu kuleana e nānā i nā moʻolelo aupuni

ʻO ka Uniform Information Practices Act (HRS Mokuna 92F) — i kapa ʻia ʻo UIPA — ka māhele kānāwai moʻolelo lehulehu o Hawaiʻi. E noʻonoʻo iā ia he mea like me ka Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) o ka paeʻāina, akā no ka aupuni mokuʻāina a me ka aupuni kalana.

Ma ka ʻōlelo maʻalahi: hiki iā ʻoe ke noi i kekahi ʻoihana mokuʻāina a i ʻole ʻoihana kalana no kā lākou mau moʻolelo, a pono lākou e hāʻawi iā ʻoe — ke ʻole kekahi ʻokoʻa kānāwai kikoʻī e kū ana.

Ka mea hiki iā ʻoe ke noi

ʻO hapa nui o nā moʻolelo aupuni. Aia ma kēia nā palapala, nā leka uila, nā hōʻike, nā ʻikepili, nā moʻolelo hālāwai, nā ʻaelike, nā leka, a me nā mea ʻē aʻe. Inā ua hana a i ʻole ua loaʻa i kekahi ʻoihana aupuni ia mea ma ke ʻano o kā lākou hana, ʻo ia paha he moʻolelo lehulehu.

Pehea e hana ai i kahi noi

E kākau i palapala. Maikaʻi ka leka uila — ʻaʻole pono kahi palapala kūikawā. E ʻōlelo pono nui ana e pili ana i ka mea āu e ʻimi nei.

E hoʻokomo:

  • Kāu inoa a me kāu wahi leka uila
  • He wehewehe o nā moʻolelo āu e makemake nei
  • He lāpana manawa, inā pili
  • Pehea āu e makemake ai e loaʻa iā ʻoe (ʻO ka leka uila ka wikiwiki loa)

He laʻana noi moʻolelo

Hiki iā ʻoe ke kope a hoʻololi i kēia laʻana:

Subject: UIPA Records Request

Dear [Agency Name],

Under the Uniform Information Practices Act (HRS Chapter 92F),
I am requesting the following records:

[Describe what you want — for example: "Meeting minutes from
the Board of Water Supply for January through March 2026"]

Please provide these records by email if possible.

Thank you,
[Your Name]
[Your Email]

ʻAʻole pono ʻoe e wehewehe i ke kumu o kāu makemake no kekahi moʻolelo. ʻAʻole pono kekahi loio. ʻAʻole pono e helu ʻoe i kekahi māhele kānāwai kikoʻī. He leka uila pōkole wale nō ka pono.

Nā uku

Hiki i nā ʻoihana ke uku aku i nā uku kope kūpono no nā kope pepa, akā ʻaʻole hiki iā lākou ke uku aku iā ʻoe no nā moʻolelo i hāʻawi ʻia ma ka leka uila. Inā e noi ana ʻoe i ka hāʻawi ʻia ma ka leka uila, ʻaʻohe pono e uku.

He aha ke hana inā ua hōʻole ʻia kāu noi?

Inā hōʻole kekahi ʻoihana i kāu noi — a i ʻole ʻaʻole ia e pane ana i ka manawa kūpono — hiki iā ʻoe ke ʻapīlī i ka Office of Information Practices. E nānā ana ʻo OIP i ka hōʻole ʻana a e hoʻopuka ana i kahi manaʻo pilikino. He mea manuahi kēia kaʻina hana.

Ka Office of Information Practices (OIP)

ʻO OIP ka hale aupuni mokuʻāina nāna e mālama ana i ka Sunshine Law a me ka UIPA. ʻO lākou ka poʻe e kelepona aku ai ke hewa kekahi mea.

Ka hana a OIP

  • Hāʻawi alakaʻi i nā ʻoihana a me ka lehulehu no nā hālāwai wehe a me ke komo ʻana i nā moʻolelo
  • Hoʻopuka nā manaʻo pilikino ke kū aʻe nā hoʻopaʻapaʻa
  • Aʻo i nā limahana aupuni
  • Paʻi i nā hōʻike makahiki no ka ākea aupuni
  • Mālama i ka Records Report System — he waihona huli i loko o nā ʻano moʻolelo aupuni ma luna o 26,000

Ka wā e hoʻokamaʻāina ai i OIP

  • Ua hōʻole ʻia a i ʻole ua nānā ʻole ʻia kāu noi moʻolelo
  • Ua pani ʻia kahi hālāwai i ka lehulehu me ka ʻole o ke kumu kānāwai
  • Ua kūkākūkā ʻia kahi kumuhana papa hana me ka ʻole o ka ʻōlelo aʻo kūpono
  • He nīnau kāu no kāu mau kuleana ma lalo o kēlā a me kēia māhele kānāwai

E hoʻokamaʻāina iā OIP

  • Kelepona: (808) 586-1400
  • Leka uila: oip@hawaii.gov
  • Pūnaewele: oip.hawaii.gov

Nā kumu ʻikepili ākea aupuni ʻē aʻe

Ma waho o nā hālāwai wehe a me nā moʻolelo lehulehu, kōkua nā hale ʻē aʻe iā ʻoe e hahai ana i ke kālā, e nānā ana i nā koho pāloka, a e komo ana i nā ʻikepili aupuni.

  • Hawaii State Ethics Commission — Nā hōʻike kālā, nā inoa loio hoʻonaʻauao, a me ke alakaʻi hoʻopaʻapaʻa pono. Kelepona: (808) 587-0460
  • Campaign Spending Commission — Nā hāʻawi a me nā lilo koho pāloka no nā moho mokuʻāina a me nā moho kalana
  • Hawaii State Legislature — Nā pila kānāwai, nā papa hana hālāwai, a me ke waiho ʻana i ka ʻōlelo hōʻike
  • eHawaii Public Meeting Calendar — Ka papa hana hālāwai pono o ka mokuʻāina
  • Office of Elections — Ka inoa koho pāloka, nā lā koho, a me nā hopena

Nā kumu ʻikepili aupuni ma kēlā me kēia kalana

He kaunihela, nā papa, a me nā komisina kona iho o kēlā me kēia kalana. Aia ma ʻaneʻi nā wahi e hoʻomaka ai:

  • Honolulu City Council — Ka City & County o Honolulu (Oʻahu)
  • Maui County Council — Maui, Molokaʻi, a me Lānaʻi
  • Hawaii County Council — Ka Mokupuni Nui
  • Kauai County Council — Kauaʻi a me Niʻihau

He papa a komisina kona iho o kēlā me kēia kalana. E nānā i ka papa inoa hālāwai civi.me no ka papa inoa piha.
