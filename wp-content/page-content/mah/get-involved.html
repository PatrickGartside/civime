Boki juon civi.me Ambassador

Ambassador ro rej buto eo an civi.me ilo community. Rej host-i event ko, share-i resource ko, im jiban jiran ko aer understand ewi wawein participate ilo public decision ko.

Men in ej juon volunteer role — ejjelok experience en aikuj. Elane kwoj care kin am community im kwoj konaan jiban armij ro engage, kwoj qualified.

Ta ambassador ro rej komane

  • Host-i letter-writing party ko im civic engagement event ko ilo am neighborhood
  • Share-i civi.me tool ko im resource ko ibben jera, baamle, im community group ko
  • Lewoj feedback eo ej shape-i ewi wawein civi.me ej develop
  • Connect-i kim nan community ko kim jab reach er wot

Won kim ej etale

Kim ej bwe elaptata konaan rongorong jen high school im college student ro, community organizer ro, neighborhood board member ro, im jabdewot armij ej active ilo juon cultural ak community organization. Ejjelok prior civic experience en aikuj — enthusiasm wot im juon connection nan am community.

Kim ej build-i partnership ko ibben UH Manoa, community college ko, im high school service-learning program ko. Elane kwe juon student ej etale juon meaningful project, men in ej juon fit emman.

Ta kwoj boki
