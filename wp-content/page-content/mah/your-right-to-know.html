Sunshine Law — am right nan attend-i public meeting ko

Sunshine Law (Hawaii Revised Statutes Chapter 92) ej Hawaii eo an open meetings law. Ej ba bwe naat juon government board, commission, ak council ej meet nan kenono kin public business, kwoj ewor right nan bed ie.

Ta Sunshine Law ej require

  • Notice mokta. Meeting agenda ko rej aikuj post at least jiljino raan mokta jen meeting eo.
  • Agenda ko rej publish. Notice eo ej aikuj list-i kajojo item nan kenono kaki. Juon board ej jab maaron kenono kin mein eo ejjelok ilo agenda eo.
  • Public testimony. Kwoj ewor right nan testify ilo agenda item ko ilo public meeting ko.
  • Public vote ko. Vote ko rej aikuj walok ilo open. Ejjelok secret ballot.
  • Minute ko rej publish. Board eo ej aikuj keep-i juon written record kin ta ear walok, im record eo ej public.

Ta Sunshine Law ejab cover

  • Informal gathering ko ia ejjelok decision ko rej koman im jidik jen quorum (majority an member ro) ej bed
  • Internal staff meeting ko — law eo ej apply nan board im commission ko, jab day-to-day agency operation ko
  • Legislature eo — Hawaii State Legislature ej operate iturin an mwiin rule ko, jab Sunshine Law (ak legislative hearing ko rej bed open nan public)

Won ej enforce-i

Office of Information Practices (OIP) ej administer-i Sunshine Law. Elane kwoj believe bwe juon meeting ear improperly close nan public, ak juon agenda item ear kenono kaki ejjelok proper notice, kwoj maaron contact-i OIP (lale iturin).

UIPA — am right nan loe government record ko

Uniform Information Practices Act (HRS Chapter 92F) — etan UIPA — ej Hawaii eo an public records law. Lomnak kaki einwot Hawaii eo an version an federal Freedom of Information Act (FOIA), ak nan state im county government.

Ilo naan ko rejemman: kwoj maaron kajjitok jabdewot state ak county agency nan aer record ko, im rej aikuj lewoj kwe — elane ejjelok specific legal exemption ej apply.

Ta kwoj maaron request

Bobrae aolep government record. Men in ej include document ko, email ko, report ko, data, meeting minute ko, contract ko, correspondence, im elap lok. Elane juon government agency ear create-i ak boki einwot part an aer jerbal, men eo ej likely juon public record.

Ewi wawein koman juon request

Jeje. Email ej jerbal emman — ejjelok special form en aikuj. Bwe specific jonan kwoj maaron kin ta kwoj etale.

Include-i:

  • Am eta im email address
  • Juon description kin record ko kwoj konaan
  • Juon date range, elane ej relevant
  • Ewi wawein kwoj konaan boki er (email ej emman tata)

Sample records request

Kwoj maaron copy im adapt-i waanjonak in:

Subject: UIPA Records Request

Yokwe [Agency Name],

Iturin Uniform Information Practices Act (HRS Chapter 92F),
ij request-i record ko rein:

[Kwalok ta kwoj konaan — nan waanjonak: "Meeting minute ko jen
Board of Water Supply nan January nan March 2026"]

Jouj im lewoj record ko rein ilo email elane emaaron.

Kommol,
[Am Eta]
[Am Email]

Kwoj jab aikuj kwalok etke kwoj konaan juon record. Kwoj jab aikuj juon lawyer. Kwoj jab aikuj cite-i juon specific law. Juon email eo ejidik ej aolep wot.

Fee ko

Agency ko remaaron charge-i reasonable copying fee ko nan paper copy ko, ak rej jab maaron charge-i kwe nan record ko rej deliver ilo email. Elane kwoj request email delivery, ejab aikuj ewor cost.

Elane am request ej deny?

Elane juon agency ej refuse-i am request — ak ejab respond iloan juon reasonable time — kwoj maaron appeal nan Office of Information Practices. OIP naaj review-i denial eo im issue-i juon formal opinion. Process in ej free.

Office of Information Practices (OIP)

OIP ej state office eo ej administer-i Sunshine Law im UIPA ruo. Rej armij ro nan call naat mein ko rejab jerbal einwot ej aikuj.

Ta OIP ej komane

  • Ej lewoj guidance nan agency ko im public kin open meeting ko im records access
  • Ej issue-i formal opinion ko naat dispute ko rej walok
  • Ej conduct-i training nan government employee ro
  • Ej publish-i annual report ko kin government transparency
  • Ej maintain-i Records Report System — juon searchable database kin elonlok jen 26,000 kain government record

Naat nan contact-i OIP

  • Am records request ear deny ak ignore
  • Juon meeting ear close nan public ejjelok legal justification
  • Juon agenda item ear kenono kaki ejjelok proper notice
  • Ewor am juon kajjitok kin am right ko iturin law ko ruo
