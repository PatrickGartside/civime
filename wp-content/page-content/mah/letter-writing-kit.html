Jeje nan juon representative ej juon an ko ial ko rediktata nan participate ilo public decision ko. Kit in ej lewoj eok tool ko nan komane: ewi wawein lo won nan jeje nan, template ko nan jino, im instruction ko nan submit-i am letter ilo ial ko rebojak.

civi.me ejab kwalok eok ta position nan boki. Template ko rein rej neutral — rej lewoj eok juon structure im kwe kwoj fill-i kab am mwiin ainikien, am mwiin experience, im am mwiin lale.

Step 1: Lo won ej represent-i kwe

Hawaii ewor jet layer in representation. Depand ilo am address, kwoj maaron ewor:

  • Juon State Senator im State Representative ilo Hawaii State Legislature
  • Juon County Council member (Honolulu, Maui, Hawaii County, ak Kauai County)
  • Juon Neighborhood Board member (Honolulu wot — 33 neighborhood board rej cover Oahu)
  • Representation ilo specific board im commission ko rej relevant nan am issue

Ewi wawein lo am state legislator ro

  • Etal nan capitol.hawaii.gov — Find Your Legislator
  • Enter-i am home address
  • Kwoj naaj loe am State Senator im State Representative kab aer contact information

Ewi wawein lo am county council member

  • Honolulu: honolulucitycouncil.org — Koba district map eo nan lo am council district
  • Maui County: mauicounty.gov/council
  • Hawaii County: hawaiicounty.gov/departments/council
  • Kauai County: kauai.gov — County Council

Ewi wawein lo am Honolulu Neighborhood Board

  • Etal nan honolulu.gov — Neighborhood Commission
  • Lo am neighborhood ilo list eo an 33 board
  • Neighborhood board meeting ko rej open nan aolep ri-jokwe — kwoj jab aikuj jeje mokta nan attend

Step 2: Kojerbal juon template

Template ko rein rej lewoj eok juon structure nan jino. Replace-i aolep mein ilo bracket ko kab am mwiin information. Add-i am mwiin detail ko — etke issue in ej matter nan kwe, ta kwoj observe ilo am community, ak ta outcome kwoj hope kaki. Personal letter ko rej elaplok effective jen generic ko.

Template A: General letter nan juon representative

[Raan]

[Etan Representative]
[Title — e.g., State Senator, Council Member]
[Office Address]

Yokwe [Etan Representative],

Eta eo ao ej [Am Eta] im ij juon ri-jokwe in [am neighborhood ak district]. Ij jeje nan share-i ao lale kin [topic ak issue].

[Ilo am mwiin naan: kwalok ta kwoj understand kin issue eo. Ta ej walok? Ta decision eo rej komane? Naat?]

[Ilo am mwiin naan: kwalok etke men in ej matter nan kwe ak am community. Ta kwoj observe? Ta concern ko am, ak ta kwoj support?]

Ij respectfully kajjitok bwe kwoj [kwalok action eo kwoj konaan ren boki — attend-i juon hearing, vote juon wawein, kajjitok elaplok information, hold-i juon community meeting, im elap lok].

Kommol nan am iien im nan am service nan ad community. Ij kottale nan rongorong jen kwe.

Kab ao lale emman,
[Am Eta]
[Am Address]
[Am Email ak Phone — ejjelok aikuj, ak ej jiban elane kwoj konaan juon response]

Step 3: Submit-i am letter

Email

Email ej ial eo emman tata im eo ej common tata. Elaptata legislator ro im council member ro rej list juon public email address ilo aer official page. Koba juon clear subject line: "Constituent letter kin [topic]" ak "Written testimony — [bill number ak agenda item]."

Mail

Physical letter ko remaroon effective, bwe elaptata nan state legislator ro. Address-i envelope eo nan representative eo an Capitol ak district office. Koba address eo ej list ilo aer official page. Allow-i extra iien nan physical mail — jab mail-i raan eo mokta jen juon hearing.

Written testimony nan Hawaii Legislature hearing ko

  • Etal nan capitol.hawaii.gov
  • Lo bill eo ak hearing eo kwoj konaan comment kaki
  • Etale juon "Submit Testimony" link ilo hearing eo ak measure page eo
  • Written testimony ej usually due 24 awa mokta jen hearing eo
  • Aolep testimony ej submit ej boki part an public record

County council written comment ko

  • Kajojo county ewor an mwiin submission process — check-i specific council website eo nan instruction ko
  • Nan Honolulu, written testimony ej usually maaron submit ilo email nan council clerk eo mokta jen meeting eo
  • Deadline ko rej vary — check-i specific agenda eo nan submission cutoff iien ko

Public comment period ko

  • State agency ko rej post proposed rule ko im environmental document ko nan public comment
  • Comment period ko rej usually 30 nan 45 raan im rej publish ilo Hawaii Administrative Rules im official notice ko
  • Submit ilo ial eo ej list ilo specific notice eo — usually email ak mail nan agency eo

Tip ko nan effective letter ko

  • Bwe specific. "Ij jokwe jilu block jen proposed development site eo im eini ta iloe" ej elaplok powerful jen vague general statement ko.
  • Bwe concise. Juon nan ruo page ej enough. Representative ro rej boki elap letter ko — juon focused letter ej elaplok likely nan riiti aolep.
  • Bwe respectful. Menan kwoj disagree strongly, juon respectful tone ej elaplok effective. Representative ro rej armij.
  • Sign-i am eta im include-i am address. Anonymous letter ko rej jidik aer weight. Elane kwoj jeje nan juon state legislator, rej aikuj jela bwe kwe juon constituent ilo aer district.
  • Jeje am mwiin letter. Form letter ko rej count ak individual letter ko rej share-i personal experience rej elaplok impactful.
  • Jilkinlok ilo iien. Nan hearing ko, submit-i written testimony at least 24 awa mokta. Nan public comment period ko, jab kottale ae raan eo jemlok.

Boki elaplok jiban

Elane kwoj konaan jiban nan lo representative eo emman ak understand-i submission process eo nan juon specific issue, reach out nan kim. Kim ej happy nan jiban.
