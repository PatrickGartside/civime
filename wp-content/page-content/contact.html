Common questions

I want to become an ambassador

Email us at hello@civi.me with the subject "Ambassador Program" — or visit the Get Involved page to learn more about what ambassadors do.

I want to contribute code

civi.me is open source. Start by browsing our GitHub repository, or email us if you want to discuss a contribution idea before diving in.

I want to partner with civi.me

We work with libraries, community centers, cultural organizations, universities, and nonprofits. Email us at hello@civi.me with the subject "Partnership."

I found a bug or have a feature request

You can open an issue on GitHub or email us directly. Either way works.

About civi.me

civi.me is a project of Access100, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit based in Honolulu, Hawaii. Our mission is to make civic participation accessible to every resident of Hawaii.
