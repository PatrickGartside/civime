ʻOku ʻoatu ʻe he lao ʻo Hawaii ha ngaahi totonu makehe ke ke sio ki he ngaahi lekooti puleʻanga pea ʻaʻahi ki he ngaahi fakataha fakafonua. Ko e lao ʻe ua ʻoku na fakatuʻunga e fakamatala fakasīsolo ʻa e puleʻanga ʻi he siteiti — pea ko e ongo lao ko ia ʻoku puleʻi ʻe he ʻōfisi tatau. Ko e peesi ni ʻoku ne fakamatalaʻi pe ko e hā kinaua, founga ke ngāueʻaki, pea ko e hā ke fai ʻi he taimi ʻoku hala ai e ngaahi meʻa.

Ko ha fakamatala fakalesoni eni ʻi he lea faingofua. Ki he tohi lao kakato, vakai ki he ngaahi lao kuo fehokotaki ʻi lalo. Ki he tokoni ki ha tuʻunga makehe, fetuʻutaki ki he ʻŌfisi ʻo e Ngaahi Founga Fakamatala.

Ko e Lao ʻo e Laʻā — ko hoʻo totonu ke ʻaʻahi ki he ngaahi fakataha fakafonua

Ko e Lao ʻo e Laʻā (Hawaii Revised Statutes Vahe 92) ko e lao fakataha ava ʻa Hawaii. ʻOku ne pehē ʻi he taimi ʻoku fakataha ai ha poate, komisiona, pe kōsolo fakapuleʻanga ke talanoa ki he ngaahi meʻa fakafonua, ʻoku ʻi ai hoʻo totonu ke ke ʻi ai.

Ko e hā ʻoku fiemaʻu ʻe he Lao ʻo e Laʻā

  • Fakaʻilo kimuʻa. Kuo pau ke tuku e ngaahi polokalama fakataha ʻi he ʻaho ʻe ono ofi kimuʻa he fakataha.
  • Ngaahi polokalama kuo fakahā. Kuo pau ke lisi e fakaʻilo e meʻa kotoa ʻe talanoa ki ai. ʻOku ʻikai lava ke talanoa ha poate ki ha meʻa naʻe ʻikai ʻi he polokalama.
  • Fakamoʻoni fakafonua. ʻOku ʻi ai hoʻo totonu ke fakamoʻoni ki he ngaahi meʻa polokalama ʻi he ngaahi fakataha fakafonua.
  • Ngaahi palōte fakafonua. Kuo pau ke fai e ngaahi palōte ʻi he ʻātā. Taʻe ha palōte fufū.
  • Ngaahi miniti kuo fakahā. Kuo pau ke tauhi ʻe he poate ha lekooti tohi ʻo e meʻa naʻe hoko, pea ko e lekooti ko ia ʻoku fakafonua.

Ko e hā ʻoku ʻikai kōvā ʻe he Lao ʻo e Laʻā

  • Ngaahi fakataha taʻefakamalumalu ʻoku ʻikai fai ai ha tuʻutuʻuni pea siʻi hifo ʻi he koalamu (tokolahi ʻo e kau mēmipa) ʻoku ʻi ai
  • Ngaahi fakataha ngāue loto — ko e lao ʻoku ngāue ki he ngaahi poate mo e komisiona, kae ʻoua ko e ngaahi ngāue faka-ʻaho ʻa e potungāue
  • Ko e Fale Alea — ko e Fale Alea ʻo e Siteiti ʻo Hawaii ʻoku ngāue ia ʻi heʻene ngaahi tuʻutuʻuni pē ʻoʻona, kae ʻoua ko e Lao ʻo e Laʻā (neongo ko e ngaahi fanongonongo fale alea ʻoku kei ava ki he kakai)

Ko hai ʻoku ne fakahoko ia

Ko e ʻŌfisi ʻo e Ngaahi Founga Fakamatala (OIP) ʻoku ne puleʻi e Lao ʻo e Laʻā. Kapau ʻoku ke tui naʻe tāpuni taʻetotonu ha fakataha ki he kakai, pe naʻe talanoa ha meʻa polokalama taʻe ha fakaʻilo totonu, ʻoku lava ke ke fetuʻutaki ki he OIP (vakai ʻi lalo).

Fie sio ki he Lao ʻo e Laʻā ʻi he ngāue? Lau ʻemau fakahinohino ki he ʻaʻahi ki ha fakataha fakafonua.

UIPA — ko hoʻo totonu ke sio ki he ngaahi lekooti puleʻanga

Ko e Lao Fakataha ki he Ngaahi Founga Fakamatala (HRS Vahe 92F) — ʻoku ui ko e UIPA — ko e lao lekooti fakafonua ʻa Hawaii. Fakakaukau ki ai ko e tatau ʻi Hawaii ʻo e Lao Tauʻatāina ʻo e Fakamatala fēteli (FOIA), ka ki he puleʻanga siteiti mo e konitī.

ʻI he lea faingofua: ʻoku lava ke ke kole ki ha potungāue siteiti pe konitī honau ngaahi lekooti, pea kuo pau ke nau ʻoatu kiate koe — kae ʻoua pe ʻoku ngāue ha fakatauʻatāina fakalao makehe.

Ko e hā ʻoku lava ke ke kole

Ko e lekooti puleʻanga ʻoku meimei kotoa. ʻOku kau ki ai e ngaahi tohi, ngaahi īmeili, ngaahi lipooti, fakamatala, miniti fakataha, ngaahi aleapau, ngaahi tohi, mo e ngaahi meʻa kehe. Kapau naʻe faʻu pe maʻu ia ʻe ha potungāue puleʻanga ko e konga ʻo ʻenau ngāue, ʻoku ngali ko ha lekooti fakafonua ia.

Founga ke fai ai ha kole

Tohi ia. ʻOku ngāue e īmeili — ʻoku ʻikai fiemaʻu ha foomu makehe. Ngāue ke ke mahino ange ʻi he meʻa ʻoku ke kumi ki ai.

Kau ki ai:

  • Ho hingoa mo ho tuʻasila īmeili
  • Ha fakamatala ki he ngaahi lekooti ʻoku ke fiemaʻu
  • Ha fakangatangata ʻaho, kapau ʻoku fekauʻaki
  • Founga ʻoku ke fie maʻu ai kinautolu (ko e īmeili ʻoku vave taha)

Ngaahi totongi

ʻE lava ke totongi ʻe he ngaahi potungāue ha totongi hiki angamaheni ki he ngaahi tatau pepa, ka ʻoku ʻikai lava ke nau totongi koe ki he ngaahi lekooti ʻoku ʻoatu ʻi he īmeili. Kapau te ke kole ke ʻoatu ʻi he īmeili, ʻoku totonu ke ʻikai ha fakamole.

Kapau ʻoku taʻofi hoʻo kole?

Kapau ʻoku taʻofi ʻe ha potungāue hoʻo kole — pe ʻoku ʻikai tali ʻi ha taimi feʻunga — ʻoku lava ke ke tangi ki he ʻŌfisi ʻo e Ngaahi Founga Fakamatala. ʻE sivi ʻe he OIP e taʻofi pea ʻoatu ha vakai faka-ʻōfisi. Ko e founga ni ʻoku taʻetotongi.

Ko e ʻŌfisi ʻo e Ngaahi Founga Fakamatala (OIP)

Ko e OIP ko e ʻōfisi siteiti ia ʻoku ne puleʻi e Lao ʻo e Laʻā mo e UIPA fakatouʻosi. Ko kinautolu ia ke ui ki ai ʻi he taimi ʻoku ʻikai ngāue totonu ai ha meʻa.

Ko e hā ʻoku fai ʻe he OIP

  • ʻOatu ha fakahinohino ki he ngaahi potungāue mo e kakai fekauʻaki mo e fakataha ava mo e maʻu lekooti
  • ʻOatu ha ngaahi vakai faka-ʻōfisi ʻi he taimi ʻoku tupu ai ha ngaahi fepaki
  • Fakahoko ha akoʻanga ki he kau ngāue puleʻanga
  • Fakahā ha ngaahi lipooti fakatau fekauʻaki mo e fakamatala fakasīsolo ʻa e puleʻanga
  • Tauhi e Sisitemi Lipooti Lekooti — ko ha ʻūluaki fakamatala ʻoku lava ke fekumi ʻoku laka hake ʻi he faʻahinga lekooti puleʻanga ʻe 26,000

ʻI he taimi fē ke fetuʻutaki ai ki he OIP

  • Naʻe taʻofi pe liʻaki hoʻo kole lekooti
  • Naʻe tāpuni ha fakataha ki he kakai taʻe ha ʻuhinga fakalao
  • Naʻe talanoa ha meʻa polokalama taʻe ha fakaʻilo totonu
  • ʻOku ʻi ai haʻo fehuʻi fekauʻaki mo hoʻo ngaahi totonu ʻi lalo ʻi he ongo lao

Ngaahi maʻuʻanga tokoni kehe ki he fakamatala fakasīsolo puleʻanga

Ko e meʻa lahi ange ʻi he ngaahi fakataha ava mo e ngaahi lekooti fakafonua, ko e ngaahi ʻōfisi kehe ʻoku nau tokoni koe ke muimui ki he paʻanga, muimui ki he fili, mo e maʻu e fakamatala puleʻanga.

  • Komisiona Faka-ʻEtika ʻo e Siteiti ʻo Hawaii — Ngaahi fakahā paʻanga, lesisita loipīsita, mo e fakahinohino ki he fepaki meʻa tokanga. Telefoni: (808) 587-0460
  • Komisiona Fakamole Feinga — Ngaahi tokoni paʻanga feinga mo e fakamole ki he kau kaniteiti siteiti mo konitī
  • Fale Alea ʻo e Siteiti ʻo Hawaii — Ngaahi lao, ngaahi taimi fanongonongo, mo e fakahū fakamoʻoni
  • Tohi Taimi Fakataha Fakafonua ʻa eHawaii — Tohi taimi fakataha faka-ʻōfisi ʻo e siteiti
  • ʻŌfisi ʻo e Ngaahi Fili — Lesisita palōte, ngaahi ʻaho fili, mo e ngaahi ola

Ngaahi maʻuʻanga tokoni puleʻanga ʻi he konitī

ʻOku ʻi he konitī takitaha ʻene kōsolo, ngaahi poate, mo e ngaahi komisiona ʻoʻona. Ko e ngaahi tuʻunga kamata mahuʻinga eni:

  • Kōsolo Kolo ʻo Honolulu — Kolo & Konitī ʻo Honolulu (Oahu)
  • Kōsolo Konitī ʻo Maui — Maui, Molokai, mo Lanai
  • Kōsolo Konitī ʻo Hawaii — Big Island
  • Kōsolo Konitī ʻo Kauai — Kauai mo Niihau

ʻOku ʻi he konitī takitaha foki ʻene ngaahi poate mo e komisiona ʻoʻona. Vakai ki he fakahinohino fakataha ʻo e civi.me ki he lisi kakato.
